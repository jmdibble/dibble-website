Process.

I believe the key to a successful product is meaningful research and testing in key target market segments.

Then shipping a well planned MVP quickly and iterating based on feedback ensures a maximised product lifecycle.

Research

Identify your core users and the problems your product will solve.

Design

Design a simple MVP which takes a user from A to B. Prove your concept.

Test

Test this on a small group of users and iterate design based on their feedback.

Build

Organise teams, manage the backlog and bring the product to life.

This is not to say every product can acheive success with a cookie cutter approach.

Every product is different and every user is different. The main objective is to always remain agile.
